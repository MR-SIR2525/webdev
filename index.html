My name is Christian Blythe but I go by Andrew, my middle name. I am 21 years old as of December 31st, and I am studying to be a coder/programmer at Pearl River Community College in Forest County, Mississippi. I work regularly for my father at his mechanic shop on various gas-powered automobiles, doing anything from brakes to replacing a radiator or alternator. I also work on my car, sometimes for fun, sometimes out of necessity. Generally, I am a reserved and quiet person who values honesty and a do-it-yourself mentality.

Video Games

I have a few different activities I like to do in my free time. Video games are one thing, taking up a notable portion of my free time. My most played games currently are Rocket League (car soccer game), Minecraft (open-world building and exploration), and The Forest (wilderness survival horror game). Stock Market

Then, there's the stock market. I began learning about and investing in the stock market back in September of 2020, buying fractional shares in companies like 3M, Southwest Airlines, Tesla, and Exxon Mobil. At first, I was putting up tiny amounts of money into companies I knew a bit about. Since then, I've come a long way, having learned much of the lingo, research requirements, and risks. Now, I spend most of the time reading up on the news about companies/stocks I watch, keeping up with current events that could potentially affect the market. I also look at charts and past performance of stocks I am interested in, but I try not to spend too much time getting caught up in the day-to-day ups and downs. Overall, my goal is to use the stock market to accumulate some wealth over the long term.
