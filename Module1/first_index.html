Stock Market

Then, there's the stock market. I began learning about and investing in the stock market back in September of 2020, buying fractional shares in companies like 3M, Southwest Airlines, Tesla, and Exxon Mobil. At first, I was putting up tiny amounts of money into companies I knew a bit about. Since then, I've come a long way, having learned much of the verbiage, research requirements, and risks. Now, I spend most of the time reading up on the news about companies/stocks I watch, keeping up with current events that could potentially affect the market. I also look at charts and past performance of stocks I am interested in, but I try not to spend too much time getting caught up in the day-to-day ups and downs. Overall, my goal is to use the stock market to accumulate some wealth over the long term.
